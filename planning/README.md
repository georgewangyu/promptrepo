## Core Concepts

### Backward Planning
Start from the goal, work backwards to now.

### Scenario Planning
Plan for multiple futures, not just the expected one.

### OKRs
Objectives and Key Results — ambitious goals with measurable outcomes.

### Pareto Analysis
80% of outcomes come from 20% of efforts. Find the 20%.

## When to Use These

- Annual/quarterly planning
- Career trajectory planning
- Project roadmapping
- Resource allocation decisions
- When feeling directionless

## Quick Prompt

**For goal planning:**

```
I want to achieve [goal] in [timeframe].

Help me plan:
1. If it's 6 months from now and I succeeded, what specifically happened?
2. What are the 3 most critical milestones between now and then?
3. What's the 20% of effort that drives 80% of results here?
4. What could go wrong? (premortem)
5. What would I need to say no to in order to make this happen?
```
